**Q. Aren't we over the whole "grand theory" thing?**

A. Agree that nothing can represent everyone, but the goal is that this is **based** in there being different perspectives. Also, can pick and choose, goal is to just present things and maybe will apply maybe not.

**Q. All these new words are annoying.**

A. I agree. Especially when there's only one or two small changes from an existing concept (for example, Mythoscapes and Doxa/fields). I'm not sure how else to approach it, though. It would feel weird consistently saying "like doxa but XYZ," and it would feel even weirder redefining it on my own terms. The concept of "Power" has been contested forever, so that's okay. But anywhere else? Just to set consistency, have shorthand that can be referenced, etc.
