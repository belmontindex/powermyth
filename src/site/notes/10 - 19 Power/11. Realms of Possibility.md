What are **Realms of Possibility**? Well, you tell me. In our society, what do you think is possible?

The Realm of Possibility essentially represents the highest level of the concepts we have discussed so far. Due to the mechanisms of [[10 - 19 Power/10. Power\|10. Power]], Realms represent the social boundaries that define fields of action. Each person has their own, defined largely by the [[20 - 29 Myths/20 Power Myths/20. Power Myths\|20. Power Myths]] they are exposed to and the [[20 - 29 Myths/21 Mythoscapes/21. Mythoscapes\|21. Mythoscapes]] that define them.

[[10 - 19 Power/12. Dominant Realm\|12. Dominant Realm]], then, represents the average of a given society's Realms. [[60 - 69 Power Builders/61 Countercultures/61. Countercultures\|61. Countercultures]] and [[30 - 39 Power Weapons/32 Echocultures/32. Echocultures\|32. Echocultures]] are constantly negotiating boundaries to determine what Dominant Culture is.
