What are **Realms of Possibility**? Well, you tell me. In our society, what do you think is possible?

Realms represent the social boundaries that define possible action. They are shaped by the mechanisms of [[10 - 19 Power/10. Power\|10. Power]]. 

Each person has their own Realm of Possibility, largely defined by the [[20 - 29 Myths/21 Mythoscapes/21. Mythoscapes\|21. Mythoscapes]] they subscribe to. However, the [[10 - 19 Power/12. Dominant Realm\|12. Dominant Realm]], or the average of a given society's Realms, has a significant impact on members in that society.

Realms are maintained by [[10 - 19 Power/13. Realm Enforcers\|13. Realm Enforcers]]: namely, institutions and culture. Through the process of [[10 - 19 Power/15. Boundary Negotiation\|15. Boundary Negotiation]], they can be changed when [[10 - 19 Power/14. Conditions of Power\|14. Conditions of Power]] are used as [[30 - 39 Power Weapons/30 Power Weapons/30. Power Weapons\|30. Power Weapons]] or [[50 - 59 Power Tools/50 Power Tools/50. Power Tools\|50. Power Tools]].

The [[10 - 19 Power/12. Dominant Realm\|12. Dominant Realm]], then, represents the average of a given society's Realms. [[60 - 69 Power Builders/61 Countercultures/61. Countercultures\|61. Countercultures]] and [[30 - 39 Power Weapons/32 Echocultures/32. Echocultures\|32. Echocultures]] are constantly negotiating boundaries to determine what Dominant Culture is.
