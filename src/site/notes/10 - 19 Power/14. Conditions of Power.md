**Conditions of Power** are essentially accumulative capital (whether material or social) that serve as *potential* [[10 - 19 Power/10. Power\|10. Power]]. We're talking money, resources, policies, clout, status, and then everything that's included in the [[20 - 29 Myths/20 Power Myths/20. Power Myths\|20. Power Myths]] section. These Conditions form the base of [[30 - 39 Power Weapons/30 Power Weapons/30. Power Weapons\|30. Power Weapons]] and [[50 - 59 Power Tools/50 Power Tools/50. Power Tools\|50. Power Tools]]. Although their value is relationally determined, **more** of a Condition generally leads to an advantage for [[10 - 19 Power/15. Boundary Negotiation\|15. Boundary Negotiation]] (and vice versa).
