## Power Capacitors

**Power Capacitors** are essentially accumulative capital (whether material or social) that serve as *potential* uses of [[10 - 19 Power/10. Power\|Power]]. We're talking money, resources, policies, clout, status, and then everything that's included in the [[20 - 29 Myths/20. Myths\|Myths]] section. These Capacitors form the base of [[30 - 39 Power Weapons/30. Power Weapons\|Power Weapons]] and [[50 - 59 Power Tools/50. Power Tools\|Power Tools]]. Although their value is relationally determined, **more** Capacity leads to an advantage for [[10 - 19 Power/15. Boundary Negotiation\|Boundary Negotiation]] and can result in either [[30 - 39 Power Weapons/35. Concentration\|Concentration]] or [[30 - 39 Power Weapons/36. Deprivation\|Deprivation]].
