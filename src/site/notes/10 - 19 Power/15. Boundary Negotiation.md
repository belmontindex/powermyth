**Boundary Negotiation** is the eternal process by which [[10 - 19 Power/11. Realms of Freedom\|Realms of Freedom]] are contracted or expanded. At any given moment, [[30 - 39 Power Weapons/30. Power Weapons\|weapons]] and [[50 - 59 Power Tools/50. Power Tools\|tools]] are being employed, even sometimes unknowingly, to change what is considered possible or reasonable. 

![power shifts the boundaries](https://dillonbelmont.com/03powermyth-assets/bndry.gif)

A new state law is enacted to prevent discrimination, a debate with a friend changes a long-held opinion, or a notable expression of [[50 - 59 Power Tools/52. Art\|art]] adds a new perspective to the national conversation. All are examples of boundary negotiation, though they vary in terms of **scale** (individual, group, or societal), **type** (institutional or cultural), and **extent** (significance of contraction/expansion).
