## The Other

> [!fail] *This page is a barren wasteland. Come back soon!*

- **Perpetual Foreigner**: The idea that immigrant groups can never fully identify as American, regardless of legal status.
- **Flyover Country**: The idea that certain geographic regions are undesirable due to supposedly monolithic populations that inhabit them. *“Why would anyone go to Alabama?”*
- **Polarized Times**: The idea that divisive political ideologies must prevent human connection.
