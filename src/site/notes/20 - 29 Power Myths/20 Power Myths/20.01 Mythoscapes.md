[[20 - 29 Power Myths/20 Power Myths/20 Power Myths\|20 Power Myths]] are not always found in isolation. Sometimes, they are associated with a larger family - a *collection* of beliefs that form a **Mythoscape**. 

This is basically ideology - a system of beliefs that shape how people see the world.
- republicans always gotta believe in guns, etc.
- capitalist social contract

Adherence to a Mythoscape is generally associated with an identity. Consider, for example, the elements that come together to be an "American."
