**Actors** - individual people, groups, or entities - are vessels for [[10 - 19 Power/10. Power\|10. Power]] in different ways. Their role in society serves certain strengths and weaknesses for types of Power to be manifested, which is very helpful for our understanding!!

Take the [[10 - 19 Power/13. Permeative Types\|13. Permeative Types]] of Power, for example. We'll want to know how [[40 - 49 Power Actors/43 Government/43. Government\|43. Government]] is involved in shaping institutions, we'll want to know how [[40 - 49 Power Actors/44 Media/44. Media\|44. Media]] shapes culture. They all have tools unique to them! From here, we can strategize what the best avenues are for leveraging a particular type of Power.

Also, a quick note - throughout the [[10 - 19 Power/11. History of Power\|11. History of Power]], it has been very common for interpretations of Power to center on domination of others by particular Actors. This emphasizes a limited view of Actors "holding" Power and does not center the structures that cause such situations. We've shifted away from a specific *focus* on Actors, but there's still so much value in understanding them! They're still an important part of the equation!
