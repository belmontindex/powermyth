**Power Generators** are actors - individuals, groups, or entities - that illustrate the struggle to expand the [[10 - 19 Power/12. Dominant Realm\|12. Dominant Realm]] using [[50 - 59 Power Tools/50 Power Tools/50. Power Tools\|50. Power Tools]]. 

In a traditional sense, Generators are those who would be considered "powerless:" they are often oppressed, marginalized, or disadvantaged in some form by the [[10 - 19 Power/13. Realm Enforcers\|13. Realm Enforcers]], resulting in less access to [[10 - 19 Power/14. Power Capacitors\|14. Power Capacitors]]. Those excluded from the Dominant Realm are particularly interesting for our purposes, as their heightened familiarity with [[20 - 29 Myths/21 Mythoscapes/21.00. Mythoscapes\|21.00. Mythoscapes]] results in, or perhaps necessitates, unorthodox functions of [[10 - 19 Power/10. Power\|10. Power]]
